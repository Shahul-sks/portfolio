SAC3

SAC3 stands for St Anthony's College Cyber Club

Project Overview

SAC3 is a student club management application. Cyber club is one of the student club of St Anthonys College and SAC3 manage cyber club. It consist of various module i.e profile, events, documents, notice. This application helps the admin i.e president of cyber club to manage and modify and update all the details of cyber club like upcomming events, program organised by cyber club, previous years question paper etc.

Since the launch of this project, It has received good respond from the memeber of cyber club and the faculty member of Computer Science Department of St anthonys College.
